This Storybook documents the project's **tokens**, **components**, and **layouts**.  
Use the toolbar to switch **theme** (light/dark) and **locale** (en-US/pt-BR).

## Foundations

- **Tokens**: color, typography, spacing, radius.
- **Theme**: generated from tokens for MUI.

## Guidelines

- Buttons keep original capitalization (no `textTransform`).
- Rounded corners (_md/lg_) for primary surfaces and controls.
- Accessibility: open the **A11y** panel to check violations.

## Layouts

See `Layouts/AppShell` for the default navigation shell.
